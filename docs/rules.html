School Rules & Regulations

At Apna School, we believe that discipline is the foundation of success. Every student is expected to follow these simple rules to maintain harmony and learning excellence.

  • Students must arrive at school on time and attend the morning assembly regularly.
  • Uniform and ID cards must be worn daily.
  • Respect teachers, classmates, and all staff members at all times.
  • Use of mobile phones inside the school premises is strictly prohibited.
  • Keep classrooms and surroundings clean and tidy.
  • Students must complete homework and assignments on time.
  • Running or shouting in corridors is not allowed.
  • Bullying, teasing, or any form of misbehavior will not be tolerated.
  • Parents should inform the school about any absence in advance.
  • Students must participate in school activities and maintain a positive attitude.
